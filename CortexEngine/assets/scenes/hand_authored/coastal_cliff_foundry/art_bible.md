# Coastal Cliff Foundry

Cold ocean, wet basalt, and hot foundry runoff at dusk. The scene must read as a constructed industrial platform embedded into a natural cliff, not as detached boxes around a glowing plane.

Primary checks:

- Lava sits inside a trough with visible lips, side walls, posts, and rails.
- Rail bars attach to posts and never float through empty space.
- Wet rock and ocean water must be visibly cooler than the lava light.
- The hero camera must show foreground wet ledge, midground foundry channel, and background ocean/cliff.
