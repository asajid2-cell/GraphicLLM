# Desert Relic Gallery

Warm sun, cool sky fill, long shadows, stone, sand, oxidized metal, and small glass accents. This scene must avoid the old procedural-gallery read by making every object sit in a plausible ruin.

Primary checks:

- Plinths touch ground and collect sand at their bases.
- Relics are mounted or grounded, not floating.
- Palette must include warm stone, cool shadow, metal, and glass accents.
- Cameras should frame through architecture, not just show isolated props.
