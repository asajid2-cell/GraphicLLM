# Forest Creek Shrine

Natural creek scene built by hand, not by scatter. Rocks, moss, shrine, creek bank, fallen wood, and vegetation clusters should feel placed by terrain and water flow.

Primary checks:

- Creek water is bounded by banks and rocks.
- Shrine is grounded with moss, steps, and nearby stones.
- Vegetation clusters follow banks and background layers.
- Lighting is soft and directional, with visible filtered color instead of a flat green wash.
