# Neon Alley Material Market

Dense wet alley with deliberate storefronts, mounted signs, glass cases, chrome, steam, and colored light. The goal is not random neon panels; every light source needs a fixture or surface that explains it.

Primary checks:

- Signs are mounted with brackets or frames.
- Wet pavement carries reflections and grime, not a flat dark material.
- Steam/rain particles are anchored to drains, vents, or rain space.
- Colored light should separate materials without crushing the scene into one hue.
