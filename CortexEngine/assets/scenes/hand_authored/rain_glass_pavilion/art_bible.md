# Rain Glass Pavilion

Night rain, glass architecture, chrome, and puddled stone. The pavilion must feel built: every glass pane is framed, every frame touches floor or roof, and the wet ground gives visible reflections.

Primary checks:

- Glass panels have mullions and floor channels.
- Refraction/tint should not turn into a brown flat transparent wall.
- Wet floor has drains, tile seams, puddles, and low-angle highlights.
- Hero framing must show glass thickness, warm interior light, and cool exterior rain.
